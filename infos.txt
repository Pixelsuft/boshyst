IWBTB Command line args:
-DDRAW - force DDRAW
-DIB - force GDI
-D3D8 - force D3D8
-D3D9 - force D3D9
-DEBUG - window title with extra info
-MIS0 and -MIS1 (alt input?)
-R0 - silent errors
-R1 - normal errors
-VSYNC - force vsync
-NOVSYNC - disable vsync
-NOF
-NOK
-NOC
-NODS
-NOVR
-NOX

Object creation IDs:
28 - Player
106 - Bullet

Action event codes:
33 - someting with bullets (actionID = 94)
57 - Player movement

g_SystemFlags:
<0 - D3D9 mode
0x20 - DDRAW mode (or GDI?)
0x40 - VRAM
